Well, do I have the recipe for you! Dave's FAMOUS Mac & Cheese! A dish so beloved in
my household that I've been forced to make it EVERY SINGLE HOLIDAY! Slated to wow
your family, and super easy to make!

Step 1: Preparation

   Here is what you'll need to get started:
  • Deep Aluminum Baking Pan
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Boiling Pot
  • Sauce Pot
  • Wooden Slotted Spoon
  • Measuring Cup
  • Strainer
  • Stick of Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Gallon of Whole Milk
  • 2 64oz. Containers of Low Sodium Chicken Broth
  • 3 Packs of Velveeta Shells & Cheese
  • Minced Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Flour
  • Shredded Smoked Cheddar Cheese
  • Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese

These specific cheeses are optional and you can instead pick whatever cheese you like.

Step 2: Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. Add the two containers of Chicken Broth to the Boiling Pot
  3. Add 3 cups of water to the pot
  4. Set pot to boil on high heat
  5. Once you have a rolling boil, add Velveeta Shells to the pot
  6. While that is going, add two half cup sticks of butter to sauce pot
  7. after butter has fully melted, add one cup of flour to pot
  8. Stir flour evenly into mixture until it fully liquifies, keep stirring for the rest of the steps
  9. Don't forget to stir the Vevlveeta Shells in the boiling pot occasionally as well!
  10. Stir until flour becomes "Cookie Brown" then start slowly adding milk, a little at a time, stir until smooth
  11. Repeat adding milk and keep stirring until sauce has a creamy texture to your liking and level of thickness
  12. Add Velveeta cheese packets, 2 tablespoons of minced garlic, and 2 tablespoons of pepper to your sauce
  13. Mix all ingredients in thoroughly until sauce has even color
  14. Strain Velveeta Shells if they have gotten a soft slightly chewy texture, take boiling pot off heat
  15. Add your sauce to the boiling pot, and add back the Velveeta Shells as well
  16. Stir the mixture untill all shells are evenly coated
  17. Add all to the baking pan
  18. Cover everything with with your cheeses, mix until evenly coated, repeat once
  19. Place baking pan in the oven for 15 minutes or until cheese has gotten a crust on top
  20. Pull out of oven, cover in foil
  21. Let cool for 10-15 minutes
  22. Enjoy!
